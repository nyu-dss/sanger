Margaret Sanger, "Women's Naturalization Class," 29 Nov. 1911.

Published article. Source: New York Call, Nov. 29, 1911 .

Women's Naturalization Class.

Arrangements have been made by the Naturalization Aid Society to hold a Naturalization Class for Women every second and fourth Thursdays at 8 p.m., in the rooms of the Gaysville Forum, 1461 Third Avenue. The first meeting will be held December 14 and all women who are not naturalized will remember this date and join the class. It is free to you, women Comrades; bring your women friends, become naturalized and be able to use the vote when you are enfranchised.

Margaret H. Sanger, Women's Organizer.
