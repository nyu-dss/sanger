Margaret Sanger, " [Morality and Birth Control] ," n.d..

Autograph draft article. Source: Margaret Sanger Papers, Sophia Smith Collection , Margaret Sanger Microfilm: Smith College Collections S73:0120 .

No other versions or information has been found on this fragment of text.

If people are going to be immoral or promiscuous in their sex relations then at least let them protect Society & the Race from disease & offspring.

To my way of thinking the moral question is not effected by contraceptives.

Morality begins within one. Nothing outside can make any woman immoral.

If the Churches must keep her in ignorance to keep her pure & moral then they have failed in their work & had better give the job over to Science & be done with it.
