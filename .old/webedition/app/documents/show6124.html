Message from Mrs. Sanger.

Dear Dr. and Mrs. Drysdale,

The Fifth International Neo-Malthusian and Birth Control Conference is over. All of us from America agree that it has been inspiring and stimulating.

I have been greatly impressed by the splendid scientific support the Conference has had. Surely you must feel proud that your untiring labour has brought to birth such magnificent results. You may count upon the American Birth Control League to go hand in hand with you to the goal.

MARGARET SANGER
